Details for Participants of Civic Switchboard Workshop
NNIP Preconference, May 8, 2018, Atlanta, GA

Time and Place

Tuesday, May 8, from 11:30am to 5:00pm
Atlanta Regional Commission
229 Peachtree ST NE, STE 100
Atlanta, GA 30303

The Atlanta Regional Commission provides directions and travel information here.

Lunch and snacks will be provided. Lunch will include vegetarian and gluten-free options. If you have any other dietary needs, please let us know.

Agenda

11:30am - 12pm - participants arrive, lunch food is served

    we'll have lunch ready as you arrive and we'll eat together before the workshop program starts at noon

12:00pm - 12:30pm - Welcome, Introductions, Workshop Logistics

    including a brief overview of Civic Switchboard project, and brief introductions from workshop participants

12:30pm - 2:05pm - Mapping Ecosystem Activity

    why we advocate for ecosystem mapping; hands-on creation of your own ecosystem map; a chance to look at what other teams have produced; if you have done ecosystem mapping before, great! ...we'll have some prompts that will hopefully make this a fresh experience.

2:05pm – 3:00pm – Building Partnership Activity

    a brief typology of civic data roles that include libraries; teams consider a project to conduct as a team; use ecosystem map to situate your work and to identify who should be involved; teams create a worksheet capturing next steps.

3:00pm – 3:30pm - Social Break / snacks / propose and form Birds of a Feather groups.

    during break, participants consider: what you'd like to dive into with a group? what questions are most pressing for you? post ideas on wall; Civic Switchboard team will cluster topics into Birds of a Feather groups

3:30pm – 4:15pm - Birds of a Feather Sessions

    BoF sessions take place -- OK to split up members of a team for this part! Also OK to move between groups as your interest directs you.

4:15pm – 5:00pm - Wrap-up

    all back together -- what are your takeaways? what will you do when you are back home? also some logistical wrap-up and workshop evaluation.
